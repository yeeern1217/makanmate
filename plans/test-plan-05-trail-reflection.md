# Manual Test: 05 — Trail Reflection

## How to test

1. Catch 3 or more stalls from different cultural backgrounds (e.g. a Chinese hawker, a Malay warung, an Indian mamak)
2. Build a Heritage Trail from those cards
3. Open the Trail page

- [ ] A "Trail Reflection" section appears below the diversity stats
- [ ] It shows how many cultural traditions you explored (e.g. "You explored 3 cultural traditions: Chinese, Malay, Indian")
- [ ] If you explored 4 or more, it says "Incredible —" at the start
- [ ] It highlights your rarest catch by name and score
- [ ] It tells you how many of your stops were hidden gems (fewer than 25 online reviews), shown in green
- [ ] Total trail distance is shown at the bottom (e.g. "Total trail distance: 12.3 km")

4. Build a trail with stalls from only one cultural origin

- [ ] Diversity line says "1 cultural tradition" (singular)

5. Build a trail with stalls spread across cities (Penang, KL, Ipoh)

- [ ] Distance is much larger (hundreds of km)
