# Manual Test: 02 — Ingredient Lore with Web Search

## How to test

1. Open the app and go to any Pokedex page (e.g. tap a dish from your collection)
2. In the 3D ingredient view, tap on any floating ingredient

- [ ] A lore overlay appears within a few seconds
- [ ] The story text feels grounded and specific (mentions real places, trade routes, history)
- [ ] A fun fact is shown
- [ ] The origin region is specific (e.g. "Fujian Province, China" not just "China")
- [ ] Source links appear below the lore text (real URLs to articles/blogs)

3. Tap the same ingredient again

- [ ] Lore loads instantly the second time (cached)

4. Try tapping different ingredients across different dishes

- [ ] Each ingredient gets its own unique story
- [ ] No errors or blank overlays
